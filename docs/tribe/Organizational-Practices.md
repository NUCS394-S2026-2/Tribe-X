# Product Owner for Client Meetings

How is the product owner designated for the first client meetings? is it permanent or rotated? Who is it for each team in the tribe:
The Product Owner role is planned to rotate, possibly weekly, rather than being permanent. Each team in the tribe will decide internally who serves as Product Owner for a given period or client meeting. For the first client meeting, Red Team had Mercy, and Blue Team had Valary.

How are issues between teams within the tribe raised?
Issues between teams within the tribe are typically raised during team meetings and may also be documented through GitHub issues when appropriate.

What is the forum for discussion of issues between the teams?
team meetings
The primary forum for discussing issues between teams is the scheduled team meetings.

When does that happen? Who is required to be present?
Team meetings occur at planned times agreed upon by the teams. Preferably, all team members should be present whenever possible to ensure clear communication and coordination.

# Team Communication

What is the principal way to communicate between tribes? email? discord? slack? message group? What is the address or way to join this method of communication?
The principal method of communication between tribes is an iMessage group chat. Team members are added directly to the group for ongoing communication and coordination.

# Client Meeting Time

When is the client meeting each week? Is it static or does it move? who is responsible for coordinating with the client?
Client meetings will mostly take place on Thursdays or Fridays to discuss project progress and updates with the client. The schedule may vary depending on availability. Valary or anyone else will coordinate with the client each week to refine and schedule the meetings.
